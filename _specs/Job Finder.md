Job Finder

Create an app that helps a job seeker to:
Input their existing biographical materials (e.g. resume, LinkedIn, etc.)
Articulate and refine their job mandate*
What they like to do
What they are good at
What are the must haves in a new role
Finalize their CMF (Candidate Market Fit) to guide:
Align candidate’s strengths and wishes to actual roles in the market
Tune up of resume and linked in to be consistent
Help with networking:
Search strategy for target companies
Network strategy for getting warm intros at target companies based on network connections
Talking points for networking conversations/meetings
Help finding actual opportunities:
Search strategy to find appropriate job listings
Create an automated job search that updates a prioritized spreadsheet of hot/warm/cold opportunities
Articulate criteria for the sheet
Help with specific opportunities/application process
Create cover letters
Create key materials for interviews
Company/product summary
Talking points
Questions to ask 
Based both on the company and on the candidate’s must haves
Create thank you letters
Create suggested negotiation points (based on candidate’s must haves)
Provide some research on salary ranges for that type of role


*Articulating the job mandate should feel like a conversational interview.
